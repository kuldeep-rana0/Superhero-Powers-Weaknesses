Superhero Powers & Weaknesses

Superman

Powers: Super strength, flight, x-ray vision, heat vision

Weaknesses: Kryptonite, magic

Batman

Powers: Genius intellect, martial arts, gadgets

Weaknesses: Mortal, relies on tech

Wonder Woman

Powers: Super strength, flight, combat skill, lasso of truth

Weaknesses: Blades forged by gods

SpiderMan

Powers: Wall-crawling, spider sense, agility

Weaknesses: Emotionally driven, young/inexperienced
